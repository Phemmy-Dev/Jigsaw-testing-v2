    • Dr. Ibidunni Alonge

    Co-Investigator

    I earned bachelor's and advanced degrees in Physiotherapy (Orthopedics and Sports) from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria. I belong to the Orthopedics and Manual therapy group of Nigeria Society of Physiotherapy and am a member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy, UK. I have 15 years of experience as a Clinician and Researcher, working with clients having spinal and general neuromusculoskeletal concerns. My research and teaching experience include pain management, ethics, professionalism, and more.

    Presently, I work with NHS England as a Specialist Musculoskeletal physiotherapist and am exposed to comprehensive rheumatology practice in my trust. My interests include exercise prescription, especially in non-communicable diseases, wellness, physiotherapy in rare conditions, advocacy for people with disabilities, and myositis.

    I'm passionate about physical activity as a meaningful path to overall wellness and enjoy reading thoughtful books and discussing wellness topics.
